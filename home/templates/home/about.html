What is Mama's Letterbox?

Our Mission

Are you looking to meet other parents? Are you trying to spend less time in front of your screen and resdicover activities you used to enjoy in the past? Has parenting young children during a global pandemic made it challenging for you to do either? Then Mama's Letterbox is the place for you! No matter where in the world you are located, what your current family situation is, and what interests you have, here you will be able to find like-minded parents to exchange stories, ideas, and laughs via snail mail.


How Does It Work?

Anyone can sign up for a free account and create a profile. To see other members' profiles, simply sign up for our monthly membership plan. Once you sign up for a membership, you will be able to access our member database and contact members directly via email. While this platform is intended to foster offline connection, we understand that everyone has different comfort levels when it comes to sharing their mailing address. Feel free to commumicate via email first and move at your own pace. Not able to find anyone who seem like a good fit? No obligation to stay on; cancel your membership anytime by sending us a quick email, keep your profile, and feel free to return at a later point. Our commnunity is constatnly growing. For now, you can purchase a membership at a low symbolic cost of U.S. $0.50 per month. Our goal is to grow our community and help parents find like-minded penpals. To make this effort sustainable, prices will go up in the future. However, if you were with us from the beginning, your plan will remain at U.S. $0.50 for as long as you decide to stick around.


What If I Don't Find Anyone Who Seems to Be a Good Fit?

Not able to find anyone who seem like a good fit? No obligation to stay on; cancel your membership anytime, keep your profile, and feel free to return at a later point. Our commnunity is constatnly growing.


So, This Is It?

Our site is constantly evolving. To stay up-to-date about new features and community events, simply send us an email to be added to our newsletter. We promise we will send you exclusively information related to Mama's Letterbox. In the near future, we plan to introduce an on-site chat to allow initial communication right on the site, a blog featuring monthly member profiles, and -- pandemic situation permitting -- local Mama's Letterbox meet-ups for those interested in meeting their penpals or other parents who enjoy letterwriting in person.
